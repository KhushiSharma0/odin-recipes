Cheese bagel

Description

This bagel recipe is a perfect snack for anytime of the day, and oh so delicious! And did I mention, it is also super easy and takes a minute to make!

Ingredients

  • A bagel
  • Some cheese
  • Some butter

Steps

  1. Toast the bagel a bit (optional).
  2. Put butter on the bagel.
  3. Put a slice of cheese on the bagel.
  4. Microwave the bagel for 30 seconds, for a soft melt, or a minute for crispy cheese.
  5. All done! Enjoy!
